-Fellthorn Games

Fellthorn Games was a side project that Me and my friend Tim Drexler worked on. We wanted to play around with making games and flex some creativity. Below are some games we created.


Return To Fellthorn Manor

Return To Fellthorn Manor is our new game, it's a 3D interpretation of our first game "Fellthorn Manor". We are still working hard on this project in Unreal Engine 4.

Fellthorn Manor

Fellthorn Manor is an android based game created in the Unreal Engine. It's a simple puzzle game based on rooms. Each room has a puzzle that you must figure out to escape the rooms. At the end you must brun the house down to defeat the evil demons that lie within.

Traffic Gods

A game developed in Unity. The object of the game is to move the cars to their correct lane so that they can move at their designated speed. This helps free up traffic and allow everyone to get to their destination on time. This game was created from the hate of traffic.

Fire Vs Ice

A simple Unreal Engine game that brings a new twist to Pong. The user controls two paddles while the computer controls the other two. Hit the ball to change it to your team's ball to hit the blocking blocks on the field. Get the most team ball hits to win.
